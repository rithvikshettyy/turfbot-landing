Questions, answered.

Do my players need to install an app?

No. Everything happens inside WhatsApp, which your players already have. They tap a link, pick a slot and pay. Nothing to download.

How long does setup take?

Most turfs are live within 24 hours. Send us your details and we configure your slots, payment link and reminders for you.

How do payments work?

A UPI payment link is sent in the chat as soon as a slot is picked, so bookings are paid before kickoff. The money goes straight to your account.

Can I change plans or cancel later?

Yes. Plans are monthly, so you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel anytime. No lock-in.
